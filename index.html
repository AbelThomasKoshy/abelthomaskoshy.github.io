ABOUT THE TEAM

Team Voltrax Racing consists of a group of determined and hardworking Engineering students united by their passion for Racing. The team revolves around the motto of “Revolution through Innovation” and relentlessly work towards being more innovative and creative in their field. The focus of the team is uniting passionate engineers to turn their theoretical knowledge into practical skills and developing their problem solving. The team largely emphasis on becoming more knowledgeable through research and development and giving each team member a broader platform to nurture their talent. The team is a multi-talented basket of skills and has individuals specialized in Software designing and analysis, Drive train systems, Braking systems Steering systems,Micro controllers,IoT,Actuators and Controllers .




ABOUT THE EVENT


Eco kart is a National Event for students pursuing Engineering Degree / Diploma. The Competition is to design and fabricate a single passenger Kart which is operated via a battery. This is the IV season of the competition known as Eco Kart 2017. The Competition will not only help students build interest towards automobiles, but also help them incorporate Team work and management kills within themselves. The Problem Statement is such that Every team is compelled to do rigorous Hard work and think out of the box, thus , New innovations are ikely to grow in the Automobile Sector
